Welcome to Children of Life

We believe every child deserves a safe, joyful place to grow, explore, and make friends. Our program blends structured learning with free play in a warm, nurturing environment.

Creative Play

Art, sensory play, music, and hands-on activities designed to spark imagination and build fine motor skills.

Caring Staff

Experienced, background-checked caregivers who are passionate about each child's well-being and development.

Flexible Scheduling

Choose the days that work for your family. We offer 1, 2, or 3-day-per-week options with no long-term lock-in.

Hours & Schedule

We run three mornings a week, following the local school district calendar.
